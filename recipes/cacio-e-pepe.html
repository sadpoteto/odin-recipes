Cacio e Pepe

Description

A classic Italian dish, cacio e pepe translates to "cheese and pepper", short and to the point. Simple, but hardly easy to master. Often prepared in an expensive wheel of cheese, this recipe foregoes it in favor of a regular bowl.

Ingredients

  • table salt
  • 1 lb bucatini, or your favorite elongated shape of sauce transportation vessel
  • 2 cups grated Pecorino Romano
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, salt liberally.
  2. Cook your pasta al dente, stirring when necessary to prevent sticking.
  3. Add your cheese to a large heat-proof bowl, ensure there are no large lumps.
  4. Transfer your pasta to the bowl with tongs, this will carry over some of the pasta water.
  5. Add some of the remaining pasta water and stir vigorously, keep adding more liquid until a thick creamy sauce has formed.
  6. Add pepper to taste, serve quickly.
