Pecan Pie

Description

An American classic, and a great way to combine buttery pecan nuts with sugar. Great with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup light or dark high fructose corn syrup
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup plain sugar
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cups pecan nuts
  • 1 9 inch pie crust, unbaked

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Gently melt the butter in the microwave, heat with short bursts and stir.
  3. Add syrup, eggs, butter, vanilla extract and sugar into a bowl, mix with a spoon.
  4. Add the mixture to the pie crust, optionally decorate with more pecans
  5. Bake for 60-70 mins on the center rack.
  6. Let cool for 2 hours, then serve.
